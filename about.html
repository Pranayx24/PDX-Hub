PDX Hub

The Silicon Command Center

PDX Hub is the world’s most advanced browser-based ecosystem for VLSI Physical Design engineers. Our mission is to accelerate the learning curve of Tier-1 engineers by providing industrial-grade simulators, real-time STA tools, and a comprehensive CMOS-to-Tape-out academy.

1. Engineering Excellence

We believe industrial-grade knowledge should be accessible. Built with high-fidelity animations and 7nm foundry-aligned logic, PDX Hub provides the tools necessary to master complex EDA flows.

2. Our Ecosystem

From the **GDSII Visualizer** to the **Linux Terminal Simulator**, every tool is crafted to reflect the workstation experience of a professional Physical Design engineer.
