Concept: Slack represents the margin between required time and arrival time of a signal.

PD Flow: Used in every step from Synthesis to ECO to ensure sign-off timing closure.

Interview Tip: "Negative Slack in Post-Layout? Check for high local congestion or IR drop preventing cells from running at speed."
